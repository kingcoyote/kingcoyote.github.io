Copperlands

The Copperlands Name Generator takes 3 sets of words (prefix, root, suffix) and randomly combines them to form custom place names.

You can edit the prefix, root and suffix list to add or remove any words you want. Suffixes ending with :s will be merged with the prior word. For example, the word 'burg:s' would combine with 'oak' to become 'Oakburg'.
